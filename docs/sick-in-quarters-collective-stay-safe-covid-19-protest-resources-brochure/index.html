Sick in Quarters Collective “Stay Safe—COVID-19 Protest Resources” Brochure

Sick in Quarters (SiQ), a collective of disabled and chronically ill artists and activists, published this black and white brochure online in 2020 with advice for decreasing the chance of viral spread during and after direct action events. The brochure also contains information about protest rights and how to stay safe while interacting with police.

— Mara Mills
